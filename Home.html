Career

A career essay is a written piece where you describe your career ambitions, the steps you have taken so far, and your plans for the future. It is often required for scholarship applications, job applications, or academic admissions, and serves to convince the reader of your dedication and suitability for a particular field or position

Discussion

p> A discussion essay, also called a "for and against essay" or "balanced argument essay," requires you to examine two opposing viewpoints on a topic and then provide your own perspective. This type of essay is common in academic writing and IELTS Writing Task 2, often prompted with instructions like:Discuss both views and give your own opinion
